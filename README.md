## What is DANIC?

DANIC is a sovereign, high-performance Layer 1 blockchain 
built from the ground up to eliminate traditional consensus 
mechanisms entirely.

Instead of mining or staking, DANIC uses a four-layer 
cryptographic formula — the **H-Formula Chain (H1→H4)** — 
that makes every transaction mathematically self-proving.

No voting. No energy waste. Just mathematical certainty.
